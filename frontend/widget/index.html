AI Receptionist

Intelligent chat assistant for your self-care business

Quick Start

Add this single script tag to your website to embed the chat widget:

<script src="http://localhost:8000/static/chat.js" data-business-id="YOUR_BUSINESS_ID" data-api-url="http://localhost:8000"> </script>

Demo Mode: Click the chat bubble in the bottom right corner to test the widget. Make sure your backend server is running.

Features

Business Info

Hours, services, pricing, location, and policies

Natural Conversation

AI-powered responses with context retention

Customer Collection

Gather customer info for bookings and follow-ups

Customizable

Configured for your specific business vertical

Sample Questions to Try

Once connected, try asking:

  • "What are your hours?"
  • "How much is a haircut?" (for beauty businesses)
  • "Do you accept walk-ins?"
  • "What services do you offer?"
  • "What's your cancellation policy?"
